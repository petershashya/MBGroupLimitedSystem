BUSINESS SETUP AND REGISTRATION
We help clients establish businesses by conducting official company searches, registering business names, and offering legal advisory services. Our expertise covers partnership and NGO registration, ensuring compliance with statutory frameworks. Additionally, we support foreign investors with business visas and work permits, making the process smooth, lawful, and efficient for both individuals and organizations entering Tanzania’s business environment.
LEGAL AND COMPLIANCE ASSISTANCE
Our team provides legal and compliance support, including drafting and reviewing contracts, company incorporation, and BRELA registration. We assist with customs clearance to ease import and export operations. Furthermore, we manage certificate processing and renewals, helping clients remain compliant with regulations. These services safeguard businesses, reduce risks, and ensure smooth operations while meeting Tanzania’s legal requirements effectively.
LAND & PROPERTY CONSULTING
We provide expert support in land and property matters, including conducting land searches, due diligence, and acquisition consulting. Our services also cover human resource advisory, tax registration, and VAT certification. Additionally, we assist with financial planning and reporting, ensuring businesses and individuals manage property investments effectively while complying with legal and tax requirements in Tanzania.
BUSINESS FACILITATION & MARKETING
Our facilitation services help businesses grow through sales, mergers, and strategic partnerships. We support clients in acquiring building permits, purchase certificates, and import/export licenses. With added marketing connections and networking opportunities, we enable businesses to expand their presence, operate lawfully, and connect with relevant markets. These services strengthen operations and create pathways for sustainable growth.
FINANCE & TAXATION
We offer financial and taxation solutions tailored to business needs, including bookkeeping, accounting support, and preparation of feasibility studies and projection plans. Our services ensure proper record-keeping, tax compliance, and informed decision-making. With marketing links and networking strategies, we not only manage financial processes but also help businesses access opportunities that support growth and long-term sustainability.
REGULATORY LICENSING & CERTIFICATION
We assist clients in obtaining essential licenses and certifications, including Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), TIC/TiZESA, and chemical importation permits. Our services cover industrial licenses, fire and safety, transportation, and TBS standards. Additionally, we support with EWURA, OSHA certificates, and trademark registration. These services ensure businesses operate legally, safely, and in compliance with Tanzania’s regulatory frameworks.
LAND & PROPERTY CONSULTING & REAL ESTATE
We provide full real estate and property consulting solutions, including land acquisition, searches, and due diligence. Our services cover property valuation, management, and securing building permits. We also offer real estate brokerage and property sales support. With tailored investment advisory, we guide clients in maximizing returns while ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements in Tanzania.
